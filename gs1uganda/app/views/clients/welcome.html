Welcome To GS1 Standards Uganda
BENEFITS OF MEMBERSHIP SUBSCRIPTION 
GS1 Uganda (a GS1 Member Organization), is the only organization recognized and authorized to administer 
the most widely used supply chain standard system in the world, the GS1 System. Together with all the other 
member organization, our aim is to lead the design and local implementation of global standards for the improvement 
of the supply and demand chain. We hereby invite you to become a member subscriber of GS1 Uganda and 
therefore enjoy the following benefits: 
 
Benefits To Member Subscribers (General) 
As a GS1 member subscriber, you will have access to the following services: 
1. Allocation of GS1 Company Prefix Number for your company for the purpose of generating full sets of GS1 
Identification Keys such as: 
✓ Global Trade Item Number (GTIN) ✓ Global Shipment Identification Number (GSIN) 
✓ Global Location Number (GLN) ✓ Global Identification Number for Consignment (GINC) 
✓ Serial Shipping Container Code (SSCC) ✓ Global Document Type Identifier (GDTI) 
✓ Global Individual Asset Identifier (GIAI) ✓ Global Service Relation Number (GSRN) 
✓ Global Returnable Asset Identifier (GRAI) ✓ Global Coupon Number (GCN) 
2. Seminars and presentations to assist you to have a better understanding of the GS1 System. 
3. Technical advice on numbering, symbol marking and related matters. 
4. Verification of barcode symbol. 
Benefits to Manufacturers: 
1. Ability to export products to overseas market where bar codes are in use among retailers, thus enhancing 
competitiveness and productivity 
2. Immediate retrieval of sales information at the point-of-sale to schedule production and control stocks 
accordingly. 
3. Accurate and fast assessment of the sales success or failure of new products and product promotions. 
4. Utilization of symbol marking to facilitate internal operation, e.g. inventory control, warehouse 
management, order taking and goods dispatch. 
5. Minimize problems of international communication such as misspelled product names, eliminating product 
identification confusion. 
Benefits to Retailers 
1. Higher checker productivity from reduction of labor for item price marking and price changes 2.
Reduction in shrinkage 
3. Replacement of manual accounting system by scanning system 
4. Facilitation and simplification of inventory control, ordering and receiving of goods and product shelving 
Benefits to Customers 
1. Elimination of price errors at point-of-sale 
2. Quicker, more efficient checkout service 
3. Cash register displays each item and price, plus a printed itemized receipt
