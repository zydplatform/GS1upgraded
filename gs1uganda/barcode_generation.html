Barcode Image Generation

We offer barcode image generation services bassed on the VTS barcode numbers assigned to products to enable them to be sold in retail outlets.

Generated barcodes are saved in number of vector formats that makes it easy to be imported for use during creation of packaging artwork using graphic softwares.

Pricing

  • Existing Member:
    Ugshs. 25000/= + VAT per barcode for 1-4 barcodes
    Ugshs. 10000/= + VAT per barcode for 5 barcodes and above
  • None-Members:Ugshs. 10000/= + VAT per barcode
